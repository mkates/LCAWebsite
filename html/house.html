Our House: A Governor's Mansion

Where are you going to live while you’re at MIT? Imagine living in the former home of a Massachusetts governor, US Senator and direct heir of the colonial Massachusetts Bay Company. Imagine a spacious, six-story mansion in the heart of Boston. Imagine racking ‘em up on the antique pool table in our elegant wood paneled library. Imagine hanging out on our roof-deck high above the Charles River with incredible views of Cambridge, Boston and Fenway Park. Imagine bringing a date to dinner in our oval dining room modeled after the captain’s room on the legendary steamship Lusitania. Imagine yourself at Lambda Chi Alpha.
